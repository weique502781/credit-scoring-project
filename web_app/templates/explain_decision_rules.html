Understanding Decision Rules
How to Read Rules
If-Then Rule 95% Confidence
IF checking_account = 'no checking account'
AND duration ≤ 12 months
AND credit_history = 'all credits paid back duly'
THEN Credit = Good
150 samples follow this rule
Rule Components
  • Conditions: Feature-value comparisons
  • Confidence: Accuracy of the rule
  • Coverage: Number of samples matching
  • Complexity: Number of conditions
Tip: Simple rules with high confidence are more reliable and easier to understand. Complex rules may overfit to specific cases in the training data.
