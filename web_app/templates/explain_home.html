Why Model Explainability Matters
Trust & Transparency

Users need to trust AI decisions. Explainability builds confidence by showing how decisions are made.

Regulatory Compliance

Regulations like GDPR require explanations for automated decisions affecting individuals.

Debugging & Improvement

Explanations help identify model biases, errors, and areas for improvement.

Knowledge Discovery

Explanations can reveal insights about the problem domain that weren't previously known.
